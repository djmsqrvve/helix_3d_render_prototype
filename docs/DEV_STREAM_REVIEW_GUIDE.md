## üîç How to Check for Submissions

### 1. The "Hall of Heroes" View
The easiest way to see who signed in is to simply refresh the file:
- Go to: `https://github.com/[your-repo]/blob/main/HALL_OF_HEROES.md`
- If you see new names, they successfuly committed!

### 2. The "Commits" Feed
To see the actual "work" they did:
- Click the **"Commits"** link on the main GitHub page (next to the branch name).
- You will see a list of every person who clicked "Commit changes."
- **Stream Tip**: Click on a commit to show the chat exactly what lines that person added. It glows green!
